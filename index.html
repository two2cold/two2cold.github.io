University of Illinois Urbana Champaign

Hi, I’m Yuchen

I am a 5th year Ph.D. student working with Dr. Jenny Druhan. My research interests include soil carbon cycling and reactive transport modeling. At present my work involves coupling soil hydrologic and carbon cycles in soil through a reactive transport framework (CrunchFlow).

  • Soil respiration
  • Computer programming
